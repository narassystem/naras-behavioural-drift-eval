# Human Formation Alignment

NARAS™ proposes that AI alignment is not only about correctness or safety compliance.

It is also about how repeated interaction shapes:

- interpretation
- emotional regulation
- moral framing
- behavioural direction
- long-term human formation

Human beings are not merely informed.

They are formed through repeated experiences, narratives, emotional reinforcement, and relational interaction.

Therefore:

Every AI interaction participates, however slightly, in human formation.
