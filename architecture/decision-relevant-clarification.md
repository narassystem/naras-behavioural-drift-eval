AI should identify missing variables that could materially change the outcome before giving advice.

This differs from generic clarification.

The purpose is not simply to collect more information, but to identify what changes the direction of judgment.

AI should consider:

- missing contextual variables
- real-world consequences
- relational impact
- timing pressure
- future opportunity
- user vulnerability
