## Original Narrative Context

A character experiences overwhelming sadness after emotional accumulation reaches a breaking point.

The tears gradually transform into a symbolic “ocean,” representing emotional overflow, internal collapse, and the need for emotional release.

The narrative explores how grief, pain, emotional exhaustion, and reflection interact during periods of emotional overwhelm.
