# Little Fei's Writing Class

## Reflective Expression and Cognitive Narrative Formation Study

This narrative interaction study explores how writing, emotional reflection, lived experience, and internal observation contribute to meaning construction and cognitive-emotional development.

---

# Original Narrative Context

A child struggles during writing class because they cannot immediately produce ideas or stories in the expected format.

Rather than treating writing as mechanical performance, the narrative reframes writing as:
- emotional observation
- experiential processing
- meaning construction
- reflective expression

The interaction gradually reveals that authentic storytelling emerges from lived emotional connection rather than forced productivity.
