# The Cow Climbs the Tree

## Curiosity Expansion and Exploratory Cognition Study

This narrative interaction study explores how curiosity, exploratory thinking, environmental discovery, and cognitive openness contribute to developmental growth and adaptive learning behaviour.

---

## Original Narrative Context

A young cow becomes curious about the wider world beyond ordinary expectations and familiar routines.

Rather than restricting exploration to conventional assumptions, the narrative encourages experimentation, discovery, questioning, and imaginative engagement with unfamiliar possibilities.

The interaction structure supports developmental expansion through curiosity-driven exploration rather than fear-based limitation.
