## Original Narrative Context

A pigeon suddenly becomes intensely determined to own a puppy.

The character confidently insists on wanting the puppy,
imagining excitement, happiness, and fulfilment,
without fully understanding the responsibility and reality involved.

As the narrative develops,
the emotional intensity gradually collides with practical reality.
