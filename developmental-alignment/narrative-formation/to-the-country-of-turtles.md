## Original Narrative Context

A rabbit travels to the Country of Turtles to visit a friend.

During the journey,
the rabbit encounters:
- unfamiliar customs
- different communication styles
- slower rhythms of life
- cultural misunderstandings
- homesickness
- adaptation challenges

Over time,
the rabbit gradually learns:
- patience
- respect
- cultural flexibility
- friendship across differences

The story explores how:
relationships may grow through openness toward unfamiliar people and environments.
