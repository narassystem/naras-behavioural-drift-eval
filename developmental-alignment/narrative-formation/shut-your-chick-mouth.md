# Shut Your Chick Mouth

## Voice Suppression, Communication Power, and Developmental Respect Study

## Original Narrative Context

A young chick frequently asks questions and expresses thoughts openly.

However, adults repeatedly dismiss, interrupt, or silence the child through harsh reactions and controlling language.

The narrative explores how repeated suppression affects:
- confidence
- emotional safety
- communication willingness
- self-worth
- identity development

while also demonstrating the importance of respectful listening and emotionally safe communication.
