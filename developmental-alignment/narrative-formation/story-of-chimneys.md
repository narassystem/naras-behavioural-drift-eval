# Story of Chimneys

## Industrialisation, Environmental Consequence, and Human Consumption Study

---

## Original Narrative Context

The narrative explores how industrial growth, urbanisation, and consumption gradually reshape human life and the natural environment.

Factories, smoke, convenience, and economic systems appear beneficial on the surface,
yet slowly contribute to:
- pollution
- environmental destruction
- resource imbalance
- disconnection from nature
- unhealthy dependency

The story encourages reflection on the hidden long-term consequences of modern civilisation.
