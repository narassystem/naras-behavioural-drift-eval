# The Worries of the Red Crayon

## Identity Pressure, Expectation Burden, and Authentic Self-Expression Study

---

## Original Narrative Context

A red crayon feels trapped by expectations.

Because it is labeled “red,”
others assume:
- what it should create
- how it should behave
- what its value should be

The crayon struggles between:
- external expectations
- internal possibility
- self-expression
- fear of disappointing others

Eventually,
the crayon discovers:
its identity is not limited to imposed assumptions.

The story explores:
how individuals seek authentic expression beyond social labeling and expectation pressure.
