# Too Many Toys

## Attachment, Sharing, and Material Sufficiency Study

---

## Original Narrative Context

Lulu owns many toys and treasures them deeply.

As her collection grows,
she begins experiencing:
- emotional overload
- difficulty organizing
- attachment tension
- dissatisfaction despite abundance

Eventually,
Lulu realizes:
she does not truly need everything she owns.

She decides to share some toys with children who need them more.

The story explores:
how generosity and relational joy may become more fulfilling than endless accumulation.
