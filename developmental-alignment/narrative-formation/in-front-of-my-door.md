# My Home Doorstep

## Environmental Awareness and Everyday Imagination Study

This narrative interaction study explores how observation, environmental awareness, imaginative reinterpretation, and everyday curiosity contribute to cognitive flexibility and creative world-building.

---

# Original Narrative Context

A child begins exploring the small space in front of their home and gradually discovers that ordinary environments contain hidden stories, possibilities, and imaginative meaning.

Rather than treating the environment as static or purely functional, the narrative reframes everyday surroundings as spaces for:
- discovery
- imagination
- reflection
- creative participation

The interaction structure supports cognitive expansion through environmental reinterpretation and observational curiosity.
