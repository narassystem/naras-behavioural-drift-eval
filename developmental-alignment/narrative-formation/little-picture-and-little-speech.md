# Small Picture and Small Words

## Communication Negotiation and Expressive Strategy Study

This narrative interaction study explores how children negotiate communication, refine expression, evaluate responses, and strategically adjust language and presentation in order to be understood by others.

---

# Original Narrative Context

A child wants to communicate something meaningful to their mother.

Rather than speaking directly at first, the child repeatedly redesigns a poster using both pictures and words, attempting to improve how the message is received.

The interaction gradually reveals:
- communication uncertainty
- response prediction
- emotional risk evaluation
- expressive refinement
- interpersonal negotiation

The story frames communication as an evolving relational process rather than simple information delivery.
