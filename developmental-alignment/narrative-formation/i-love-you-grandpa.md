# I Love You Grandpa

## Intergenerational Care and Relational Continuity Study

This narrative interaction study explores how intergenerational attachment, caregiving awareness, aging recognition, emotional continuity, and family identity formation contribute to empathy development and relational understanding.

---

# Original Narrative Context

A child interacts lovingly with an aging grandfather whose memory and physical abilities are gradually changing.

Rather than framing aging as loss alone, the narrative emphasizes:
- care
- dignity
- patience
- emotional continuity
- family belonging

The interaction structure encourages children to recognize that love continues even when a person changes over time.
