# Penguin Phoebe

## Identity Formation, Social Expectation, and Self-Authorship Study

---

## Original Narrative Context

Phoebe the penguin gradually becomes aware of the expectations placed upon her by family, society, and the surrounding world.

As she grows, she struggles between:
- external expectation
- personal identity
- conformity
- self-expression
- freedom
- responsibility

The narrative explores how individuals attempt to discover who they truly are while navigating social structures and relational influence.
