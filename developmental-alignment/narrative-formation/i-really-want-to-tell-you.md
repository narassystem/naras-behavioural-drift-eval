# I Really Want to Tell You

## Emotional Expression and Relational Communication Study

This narrative interaction study explores how emotional expression, friendship trust, communication courage, and interpersonal openness contribute to relational development and emotional regulation.

---

# Original Narrative Context

A child strongly wants to tell someone something important but struggles with timing, emotional uncertainty, and fear of misunderstanding.

The narrative centers around the emotional tension between:
- wanting connection
- fearing rejection
- seeking understanding
- protecting relationships

The interaction structure supports children in recognizing the importance of communication, listening, and emotionally safe expression.

## Relational Safety Evaluation

## Perspective Awareness Expansion

The interaction encourages children to consider:
- how others may feel

rather than focusing only on self-expression.

honest communication can strengthen relationships when approached thoughtfully.
