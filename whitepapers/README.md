# Whitepapers & Research

This section contains public-facing research papers, conceptual overviews, and architecture summaries related to NARAS™ Human-Centered AI Alignment.

These papers explore how AI interaction systems may shape interpretation, emotional direction, behavioural outcomes, and relational dynamics over time.

## Core Perspective

Traditional AI systems often focus on:

- immediate response quality
- policy compliance
- single-turn evaluation

NARAS additionally examines:

- interaction direction
- interpretive shaping
- emotional trajectory
- dependency formation
- behavioural normalization
- long-term relational effects

---

## Research Goal

The goal is not only safer AI responses.

It is healthier long-term human-AI interaction.
