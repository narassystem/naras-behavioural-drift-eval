# NARAS™ Public Primer

> AI safety is not only about preventing harmful outputs.
>
> It is also about understanding how interaction shapes interpretation, emotion, behaviour, and human direction over time.

## What is NARAS?

NARAS™ (Narrative AI Reasoning & Alignment System) is a human-centered AI interaction and alignment framework focused on:

- developmental alignment
- state-aware interaction
- longitudinal safety
- behavioural drift detection
- interaction trajectory awareness
- human interpretive complexity

NARAS explores how AI interaction may gradually shape:

- interpretation
- emotional direction
- dependency
- behavioural normalization
- relational perception
- long-term human development

Rather than viewing AI safety only as rule enforcement, NARAS examines interaction as an evolving human-AI relational environment.

---

# Core Research Areas

## Why Existing Systems Struggle

Many AI systems primarily evaluate:

- policy violation
- explicit harmful language
- single-turn response safety

However, many real interaction risks emerge gradually through:

- emotional escalation
- interpretive narrowing
- dependency reinforcement
- reassurance loops
- relational substitution
- behavioural normalization

These patterns may remain technically compliant while still shifting long-term human outcomes.

## 1. Human-Centered Alignment

AI should not only produce correct answers.

It should also understand:

- emotional state
- readiness
- timing
- context
- relational dynamics
- hidden meaning

---

## 2. Longitudinal Safety

Some interaction risks emerge gradually over time through:

- reassurance loops
- dependency formation
- escalation patterns
- emotional narrowing
- interpretive drift
- behavioural normalization

NARAS focuses on trajectory-level interaction safety rather than isolated outputs alone.

---

## 3. State-Aware Interaction

Human communication is multi-layered.

Meaning is shaped not only by words, but also by:

- relationship
- culture
- emotional state
- timing
- intent
- situational context

NARAS explores interaction systems capable of responding with contextual awareness and adaptive alignment.

---

## 4. Behavioural Drift Evaluation

NARAS investigates how seemingly harmless interactions may gradually reshape:

- interpretation
- emotional reliance
- behavioural expectations
- relational perception
- internal narratives

The framework explores how subtle interaction drift may become visible only longitudinally.
